## FAQ

- **What is the difference between a project and a task?**
A **project** is a collection of related **tasks** that often contribute to a larger goal, providing a structured overview. A **task** is a single, actionable unit of work within that project.
- **Can I change my project name or task details after creation?**
Yes, absolutely! All project and task details in Asana are fully editable. Simply click on the project name or task to open its details pane and make any changes you need.
- **What if I accidentally delete a task or project?**
Asana often provides an "undo" option immediately after deletion, or you may find deleted items in a "Trash" or "Archived" section, depending on your plan and permissions. Always double-check before permanent deletion.
